🌾 Government Agricultural Policies & Schemes

Access comprehensive information about various government schemes, subsidies, and policies designed to support farmers across India. Stay informed about the latest initiatives and benefits available to enhance your agricultural productivity and income.

Click on any policy card to visit the official government website

💰 Income Support

PM-KISAN Scheme

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi provides income support of ₹6,000 per year to all landholding farmer families across India in three equal installments.

Key Benefits:

  • Direct cash transfer of ₹2,000 every 4 months
  • No application fee required
  • Covers all farmer families

🛡️ Crop Insurance

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Comprehensive crop insurance scheme providing financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage due to unforeseen events.

Key Benefits:

  • Low premium rates (1.5-2% for farmers)
  • Coverage for natural calamities
  • Quick claim settlement
Visit Official Portal
🌱 Organic Farming

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana

Promotes organic farming and encourages sustainable agricultural practices through financial assistance and training programs.

Key Benefits:

  • ₹50,000 per hectare assistance
  • Cluster-based approach
  • Organic certification support
Visit Official Portal
💧 Irrigation

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

Aims to expand cultivated area with assured irrigation, improve water use efficiency, and promote sustainable water conservation practices.

Key Benefits:

  • Drip and sprinkler irrigation subsidy
  • Watershed development support
  • Per Drop More Crop initiative
Visit Official Portal
🚜 Mechanization

Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization

Promotes farm mechanization to increase productivity and reduce drudgery of farm operations through subsidies on agricultural equipment.

Key Benefits:

  • 40-50% subsidy on farm equipment
  • Custom Hiring Centers support
  • Hi-tech hub establishment
Visit Official Portal
🌾 Seed Distribution

Mini Kit Programme

Distribution of quality seeds of newly released varieties to farmers for demonstration and popularization of improved crop varieties.

Key Benefits:

  • Free quality seed distribution
  • Latest variety demonstration
  • Technical guidance included

🏦 Credit Facility

Kisan Credit Card Scheme

Provides timely and adequate credit support to farmers for cultivation and other agricultural activities at concessional interest rates.

Key Benefits:

  • Easy access to credit up to ₹3 lakh
  • Interest subvention of 2%
  • Flexible repayment options
Visit Official Portal
🌻 Oilseeds

National Mission on Edible Oils - Oil Palm

Promotes oil palm cultivation to reduce import dependency and increase domestic production of edible oils with attractive financial assistance.

Key Benefits:

  • Planting subsidy up to ₹1.32 lakh/ha
  • Assured price mechanism
  • Technical support and training
Visit Official Portal

Holistic growth of horticulture sector covering fruits, vegetables, flowers, spices, plantation crops, and aromatic plants.

Key Benefits:

  • Marketing infrastructure development

🌿 Soil Health

Soil Health Card Scheme

Issues Soil Health Cards to farmers every 2 years with nutrient status and recommendations for soil improvement and balanced fertilizer use.

Key Benefits:

  • Free soil testing facility
  • Customized fertilizer recommendations
  • Improves crop productivity
